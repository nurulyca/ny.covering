About

Our Story

Once upon, I was at my lowest point because my mother passed away. I thought hard to outdo my lost and sorrow. I should pay my biggest heart-break to the most beneficial thing for my worldly and hereafter. Not long after, I decided to turn my sorrow into beautiful dresses and hijab. I found peace in the aesthetic fabrics color and I loved to see people around wearing my masterpiece of clothes and hijabs because at that time though I couldn't share my happiness since I've lost my happiness which is my beloved woman called mother.

I, a founder, was taught also that our precious time is very limited to be wasted by nothing. So I need to make a great impact for society by producing beautiful things to help women for the better way of them. Our brand is for people who willing to hijra and be a better Muslimah without feeling any burden but feeling great with their apparels and hijabs. Our colors are pretty and our fabric is comfort, as well as the best quality amongst the market. I believe many people will love wearing our products in daily repeatedly.
